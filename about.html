About Card Force

We're passionate about creating the ultimate card game experience, bringing together collectors, players, and enthusiasts from around the world.

Leadership

Ting C

Chief Executive Officer

With over a decade of experience in the gaming industry, Ting C founded Card Force with a vision to revolutionize how people discover, collect, and trade cards. His passion for innovation and community building drives our mission to create the ultimate card game platform.

Our Story

Card Force was born from a simple idea: what if collecting and trading cards could be more accessible, more exciting, and more connected? We started as a small team of card enthusiasts who believed that the magic of card games shouldn't be limited by geography or availability.

Today, we've built a platform that connects collectors worldwide, offering everything from rare vintage cards to cutting-edge custom designs. Our marketplace brings together buyers and sellers, our trading system ensures fair exchanges, and our community features help players discover new games and strategies.
